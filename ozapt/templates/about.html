Good day, ladies and gents of osu!za. Phreel here with a huge project that I’ve been planning to do ever since the end of SAOT 2. Welcome to my private osu!za Tourney. Before we get into the rules, pools and schedules, have you ever wanted to participate in my showmatches but thought they weren’t inviting enough? Well, the time might have come for you to win! If there’s anything that you are unsure about AFTER reading the entire document, feel free to @ me in #tournaments.
  1. This is a 1v1 Double Elimination Tournament.
  2. Beatmap scoring is based on ScoreV2.
  3. RO16 and Quarter-Finals are both Best of 9. 9th round being the possible TieBreaker if the scores are 4-4. Exceptions are Semi-Finals, Finals and Grand-Finals where the matches are Best of 13.
  4. The beatmaps for each round will be announced by myself in advance before the actual matches take place.
  5. If the beatmap ends in a draw, the map will be nullified and replayed.
  6. Beatmaps cannot be reused in the same match unless the map was nullified.
  7. Lag is not a valid reason to nullify a beatmap.
  8. Disconnects within 30 seconds after beatmap begin can be aborted and/or rematched. This is up to the referee’s discretion though.
  9. The maximum time a scheduled match can be postponed is 10 minutes, after that the opposition would be awarded a default win.
  10. Rescheduling of matches must be done 24 hours prior to the match time and evidence of both players agreeing to the changes must be presented to the referee who was assigned to that match.
  11. All players are supposed to keep the match running fluently and without delays. Excessive match delays coming from the player’s side can be issued with penalties such as default win or loss.
  12. Disrupting the match by foul play, picking inappropriate warmup beatmaps, insulting and provoking other players or referees, delaying the match and other misbehavior is strictly prohibited.
  13. The multiplayer chatrooms are subject to the osu! Community Rules.
  14. All players and referees must be treated with respect. Instructions of the referees and tournament management are to be followed. Decisions labelled as final are not to be objected.
  15. NoFail WILL be used on all maps with no score penalty.
  16. Players must pick at least one mod from the following list when playing FreeMod: HardRock (x1.1), Hidden (x1.06), FlashLight (x1.12), Easy (x0.5) or any of these combined.
  17. When playing the TieBreaker, mods are optional. Players may go NoMod or any mod listed for FreeMod.
  18. Players should do “!roll” right away before the warmups. Higher roll can choose to either pick first and ban next round or ban first and pick next round. The order of picking will depend on the choice of the higher roll. Both players can only ban 1 beatmap in the pools.
  19. No warmup picks will be played in the qualifiers. In bracket stages, 1 warmup pick, no longer than 4 minutes per player is permitted. If both player agree, warmups can be skipped.
  20. This tournament IS NOT restricted to South Africans only. Anyone can register if they joined the osu!za discord server before this announcement.
  21. The host and mappoolers cannot join the tournament as players.
  1. Matches and Qualifiers would take place over the weekends from Friday to Sunday. Exceptions can be issued if emergencies happen ie. funeral, COVID-19, loadshedding or feeling ill in general.
  2. The tournament will start with RO16 (Round of 16).
  3. To be eligible to register, your global rank has to be lower than 100k. Anyone below 100k can join up and compete.
  4. All players would then be seeded and grouped according to their qualifiers results and 16 players would proceed to the Round of 16.
  5. Since this is a double-elimination tournament, each player would have to have at least 2 losses to get eliminated. Players who have lost a match would carry on playing in the Losers Bracket.
  6. When the Qualifiers are being played. Each map would be played ONCE in the order of NM -> HD -> HR -> DT.
  7. Maximum of 2 minutes (120 seconds) break is allowed between each map.
  8. If there are more than 16 players then Qualifier Lobbies with different time slots would be provided for them to attend. Players can choose whichever one that suits them best.
  9. A referee will invite the players into a lobby 5 minutes before the scheduled time. If you receive no invitation, then please try to contact the referee on discord. If contacting the referee fails then @ me.
  10. If your opponent did not show up 10 minutes after the schedule time for a match, a default win would be issued.
  11. If both players did not show up 10 minutes after the scheduled time for a match, the player with a higher qualifier seeding will advance. No rematches will be tolerated within the tournament.
  12. Maximum of 2 minutes (120 seconds) to ban/pick AND to READY up is allowed.
  13. There are no restrictions on the ban/picks. Bans can be placed in the same mod pool, and maps from the same mod pool can be picked after each other.
  14. The tournament as said above starts with RO16, proceeds with Quarter-Finals, Semi-Finals, Finals and Grand-Finals. All of these rounds will have their own pools.
  15. Last minute reschedules are not permitted UNLESS you have an emergency that you can prove.
Schedules are subject to change. I’ll keep everyone updated or will try to.
